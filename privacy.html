Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Fred Fang ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the Sawari application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Microphone Access

Sawari requires access to your device's microphone to detect the pitch of your shamisen for tuning purposes.

  • Real-time Processing: The App processes audio in real-time to calculate frequency for accurate tuning.
  • No Recording: We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data or conversations.
  • No Personal Data: The audio signal is used solely for pitch detection and is discarded immediately after processing.

No Collection of Personal Information

We do not collect, track, or share any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers.

2. In-App Purchases

Sawari offers in-app purchases to unlock premium features. All purchase transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment-related data.

3. Third-Party Services

Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertisements. These ads are served by third-party advertising partners who may collect certain device information to provide relevant advertisements. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. Please refer to your advertising partners' privacy policies for more information.

Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand app usage and improve user experience. These services may collect anonymized usage data such as app crashes, feature usage, and session duration. No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach related to personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Sawari does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for all ages.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny microphone access (though this will prevent the tuning feature from working)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time

7. International Users

Sawari is available globally. If you use the App outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
